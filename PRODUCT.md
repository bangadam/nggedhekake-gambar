# Product

## Register

product

## Users

General creative users working with photos, web assets, and illustrations on desktop. They want to enlarge one local image without learning an inference engine, uploading private files, or managing technical settings.

## Product Purpose

Nggedhekaké Gambar turns the local image-upscaling job into one dependable flow: choose an image, choose 2× or 4×, upscale, inspect, and save. Success means the interface disappears into that task while color, transparency, paths, progress, and failures remain honest.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble an AI marketing site, gamer utility, Electron dashboard, or settings-heavy professional suite. Avoid neon, gradients, glass effects, oversized promotional copy, card grids, decorative animation, hidden filesystem truth, and invented controls.

## Design Principles

1. Make the primary image task obvious without onboarding.
2. Show real files, dimensions, paths, status, and failures.
3. Keep technical engine choices secondary to user intent.
4. Preserve user control at destructive or expensive transitions.
5. Prefer familiar desktop behavior over novel interaction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Every primary action must be keyboard reachable with visible focus and meaningful accessible labels. Status and errors must be announced semantically and never rely on color alone. Support reduced motion. Keep body text at least 14px and maintain WCAG AA contrast.
